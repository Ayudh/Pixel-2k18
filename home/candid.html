Photography

This is an eye catching event that encourages amateur photographers
to capture their creativity in a snap.

Rules
  • It is mandatory that all the students report at the counter with their ID cards.
  • The theme will be given to the participants on the first day of the event.
  • They will be given a day time to capture the photographs.
  • All the participants must bring their own equipment and essentials. The organizers cannot be held responsible for the loss or damage of any equipment.
  • Any kind of cheating leads to immediate dismissal of the participant from the competition.
Registrations
  • An entry fee of Rs 200 is mandatory for individual.
  • Last date for online payment is March 19,2018.
  • Participation certificates will be issued to all the canditates and Winners will be awarded prizes.
