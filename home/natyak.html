Natyakshetra

A platform where the participant or a team can compete others
with their own style of choreography and performance.

Rules
  • It is mandatory that all the students report at the counter with their ID cards.
  • Decisions made by the judges are final.
  • No arguments are entertained.
Registrations
  • Solo performance costs Rs 250 and Group performance costs Rs 500 .
  • Each group may have 2 to 10 members and each individual have to pay an amount
    of Rs 100 as entry fee at the time of issuing ID cards.
  • Last date for online payment is March 19,2018.
  • Participation certificates will be issued to all the candidates.
  • Winners will be awarded with exciting prizes.
