Technical Quiz

A mental challenge which helps participants to assess knowledge in
technical aspects and build up team spirit.

Rules
  • It is mandatory that all the students report at the counter with their ID cards.
  • Questions will be based on technical subjects.
  • Changes in rules however will be highlighted on the website. Arguments are not entertained.
  • Decision made by the judges/organisers are final.
  • Any kind of cheating leads to immediate dismissal of the participant from the competition.
Registrations
  • An entry fee of Rs 200 is mandatory for individual. Each team may have upto 3 members
  • Students may appear as a team themselves if not we will group them into teams.
  • A team consisting of students from different colleges is also allowed.
  • Last date for online payment is March 19,2018.
  • Participation certificates will be issued to all the candidates and Winners will be awarded with exciting prizes.
