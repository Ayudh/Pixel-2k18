MiniCinema

This is an eye catching event that encourages
amateur Directors to capture their creativity in a film.

Registrations
  • An entry fee of Rs 200 is mandatory for one short film.
  • Last date for online payment is March 19,2018.
  • Participation certificates will be issued to all the canditates.
  • Winners will be awarded prizes.
Rules
  • It is mandatory that all the students report at the counter with their ID cards.
  • The theme is of your choice and duration of film is 10-15 min.
    Each individual have to pay an amount of Rs 100 as entry fee at the time of issuing ID cards.
  • Short film will be presented at the time of event. So kindly bring your films.
  • Any kind of cheating leads to immediate dismissal of the participant from the competition.
  • Changes in rules however will be highlighted on the website.
