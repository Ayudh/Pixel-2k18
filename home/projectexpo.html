Project Expo

A platform where students come up with their practical projects
and show case their talents amongst the alikes.

Rules
  • It is mandatory that all the students report at the counter with their ID cards.
  • No participant is encouraged to involve in more than one project.
  • All the participants must bring their own equipment and essentials The organizers cannot be held responsible for the loss or damage of any equipment.
  • Necessity of power should be informed prior to the event and No arguments are entertained.
  • Participants may support their prototype either with the help of power point presentation or a document file.
  • Any kind of cheating leads to immediate dismissal of the participant from the competition.
Registrations
  • An entry fee of Rs 200 is mandatory for individual. Each team should have 2 to 3 members.
  • Participants should mail their abstract to pixel.jntua@gmail.com.
  • Selected participants will be intimated through mail.
  • Last date for online payment is March 19,2018.
  • Participation certificates will be issued to all the candidates.
